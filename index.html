About

AMaCA is a research project co-funded by Regione Toscana, under the framework POR FSE 2014-2020. The project scope falls within three of the technological priorities of Regione Toscana, namely future internet, services and web-based solutions and internet of things and services.

The project is funding a research fellowship ("Assegno di Ricerca", in italian) since March 1st, 2018 until February 29th, 2020. The fellowship is currently assigned to Jacopo Soldani.

Objectives

AMaCA aims at developing novel solutions to support and automate the management of multi-component applications, from design time to runtime. In this perspective, the project is structured into two operational objectives:

Novel solutions

Developing novel solutions for supporting multi-component applications, from their specification to their actual operation.

Technology transfer

Investigating how to integrate the developed solutions into existing open-source platforms
